Air Transport Throughout The World

Introduction
Throughout the years, transportation has evolved drastically from land, to air and sea.
In todays's world, air travel has dominated the world and has greatly increased our mobility. Air transport is one of the fastest modes of public transport which connects international boundaries.

It allows people from different countries to cross international boundaries and travel other countries for personal, business, medical, and tourism purposes. Billions of people now rely on air transportations for work, to see their loved ones or to simply enjoy their vacation overseas.
